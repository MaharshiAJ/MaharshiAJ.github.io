Butterfly

The butterfly valve is an assembly done on SolidWorks. The assembly consists of 3 main parts as well as a few bolts needed to attach the parts. The main housing is made by using a simple extrusion using a sketch of two concentric circles creating a hollow cylinder. From here, the arcs can be created and then patterned and mirrored onto the bottom of the model. The crank is created through extrusions as well and generating basic geometry. Once assembled the mates are created in a way such that the crank can rotate and the plate inside the housing would rotate as well however the rotation is limited to only 90 degrees.
